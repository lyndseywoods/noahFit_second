ABOUT NOAH

1st Place 2018 NPC IL State Championship Men's Physique Class D

ACE Certified Personal Trainer

Fitness Nutrition Specialist

Pain-Free Movement Specialist

ONLINE COACHING

With over 13 years of training experience, Noah Defraties has a deep passion for fitness and loves teaching others what he’s learned over the years. Noah is an ACE Certified Personal Trainer, Fitness Nutrition Specialist, Pain-Free Movement Specialist, and a 2-time NPC Men’s Physique Competitor National Qualifier. In order to be the most effective resource for his clients, Noah has personally experienced every level of the fitness spectrum, meaning he knows exactly what it takes to get to the next level regardless of where you begin. Noah’s exercise philosophy revolves around a complete understanding of fitness fundamentals. Perfecting the basics is the fastest, most reliable way to achieve results, whether that be adding lean muscle, losing body fat, or increasing overall strength.
